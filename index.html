Golf

I have been playing Golf for 5 years, from 2013 to now. I started playing back in the 4th Grade when my dad first took me to the driving range. After the first day I tried it, I was hooked and loved the sport. I currently play tournaments such as JGANC, US Kids, and JTNC. However, I have the most fun when I play a round with my dad. Our entire weekend goes to playing golf, and I try to lower my score every week. Some of my lowest scores are 77 and 79, but my main goal right now is to reach a single digit handicap. In other words, I want to start to consitently shoot in the 70's. My favorite course to play at are Redwood Canyon and San Ramon.

Speech and Debate

I have been doing Speech and Debate for 3 years and I usually do it during the summer in the Monte Vista Speech and Debate Program. In that program we can choose up to two different events and the take classes about them for two weeks. After the two weeks we have a massive tournament for every event and then there is a burger afterwards. I usually took a debate event and always took Interpretation. For the tournament I always competed in Interpretation. My first year doing it (2016) I competed against older and more experienced kids but still won first place. The year after that (2017) won 1st place again and I look foreward to competing more next year.
