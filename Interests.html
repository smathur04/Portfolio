Golf

I have been playing Golf for 5 years, from 2013 to now. I started playing back in the 4th Grade when my dad first took me to the driving range. After my first time trying it, I was hooked and have loved the sport ever since. I currently play in tournaments such as JGANC, US Kids, and JTNC. I have even placed fourth in a US Kids and JTNC game, both with a field of over 25 kids. However, I have the most fun when I play rounds with my dad. Our entire weekend goes to playing golf, and I try to lower my scores every week. Additionally, I made my highschool's Varsity Boy's Golf Team as a Freshman and played great throughout the season. Currently, some of my lowest scores are 77 and 79, but my goal is to reach a single digit handicap. In other words, I want to start to consistently shoot in the 70's. My favorite courses to play at are Redwood Canyon and San Ramon, and I hope to continue to play long after I have graduated (hopefully in College too!).

Speech and Debate

I have been doing Speech and Debate for 3 years and I usually do it during the summer in the Monte Vista Speech and Debate Program. In that program we can choose up to two different events and the take classes about them for two weeks. After the two weeks we have a massive tournament for every event and then have an award ceremony. I always take one debate event and (my favorite) Interpretation. For the tournament, I always compete in Interpretation. My first year doing it (2016) I competed against older and more experienced kids but still won first place. The year after that (2017) I won 1st place again and I look forward to competing more next year!
